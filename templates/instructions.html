Instructions for how to play:

1. To move left to right, simply press the left and right arrow keys. For example, pressing right
will move from to
2. To pick up a block, press the up arrow when directly next to and facing that block. For example, pressing up
will move from to
NOTE: You are "facing" the last direction you pressed left or right in, and by default, you begin facing right. Also, you can't pick up a block with another block on top of it.
3. To put that same block down, press the down arrow
4. To jump up on a block, press the space bar directly next to and facing that block. For example, pressing space bar
will move from to
NOTE: "Facing" as defined in the note to 2. Also, you can't jump on a block with another block on top of it.
5. When you have moved off a ledge or dropped a block off a ledge, all gameplay pauses until you reach the next lowest point.
